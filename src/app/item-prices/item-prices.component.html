Some (Tech) Examples Let's look at some popular electronics items

By examining a few products from a popular category to go on sale (electronics), we can determine whether or not Black Friday deals are truly deals.

As it turns out, not really. The lowest prices happen not during Black Friday, but actually about 1-2 months before Black Friday. Of course, some items may be exceptions to this trend, but generally the more popular items go on deeper sales during times other than Black Friday.
